Server & Community Rules

1. Conduct & Respect

Treat everyone with dignity. We have zero tolerance for toxicity, bullying, or hate speech (sexism, racism, etc.). Keep it PG-13—no NSFW or disturbing content.

No hacking, X-ray, or unfair clients. We keep the game fair for everyone. Please report any bugs or exploits to the staff instead of using them.

Respect the work of others. No destroying builds or stealing items, whether the land is claimed or unclaimed.

Do not promote other servers, Discord links, or outside websites. Avoid spamming chat to keep it readable for everyone.
